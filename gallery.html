Supporting Organizations



Hi! We're Code With Impact, a DFW-based nonprofit where high school mentors plan and host purpose-driven hackathons for elementary and middle schoolers. By blending code with creativity, participants design and pitch original apps and websites gaining coding, marketing, and communication skills –all essential in today’s tech landscape. We are inviting you to support our mission by providing a grant/sponsorship to celebrate our participants’ achievements and encourage their continued journey in tech. Together, we can nurture and amplify the next generation of leaders, innovators, and change-makers. Thank you for your consideration and we look forward to collaborating with you to uplift the youth of our community and teach them action-driven steps to create change. To further discuss supporting Code With Impact, please email codewithimpact.connect@gmail.com.
