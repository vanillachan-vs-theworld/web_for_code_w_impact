About Code With Impact

Code With Impact is a student-led organization in which high school mentors plan and host action-driven hackathons for elementary and middle school girls teaching them coding, marketing, presenting, and leaadership.



The hackathons are based on relevant social issues like financial literacy, mental health, organization, health and fitness, environmental sustainability, food supply, etc. Our goal is to provide the next generation with a platform to drive authentic change in their communities.
